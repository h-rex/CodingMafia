How To Make Money Online From Programming

Do you want to make money from programming? I think Yes, right. Now a day no one want to work 9 to 5. Everyone want to become own boss and make more money.

Programming changing the world. As you can see from last decades programming brings lots of changes in the world. Programming is the one the skill that you can learn anywhere and anytime and then the easy way to make money from coding. So let’s see some ways to make money from programming skills.

1. Blogging

Blogging is an easy way to make money as a programmer. You can earn money by sharing your programming knowledge in the blog.

Blogging is the best option for earning while learning. In a programming niche, you can share tips, tricks, and tutorials of programming. You can also start a tutorial website like GeeksForGeeks, JavaTPoint, TutorialPoints, etc.

There are many ways to make money from programming blog,

  • Through selling other’s course (promotion)
  • Affiliate marketing
  • Through Google AdSense

You just need to get enough traffic on your blog to earn. For that, you need to write SEO-friendly article. And do On-Page and Off-Page SEO for better results.

You can start your blog on WordPress and Google Blogger. You can choose one of them to start a programming blog.

A blog can take time to grow but blogging is the best way to make money from programming.

2. Develop Apps

You know already, now for everything there is an App for that right. So take advantage of programming and make money using by developing apps.

If you are familiar with Flutter, Android Studio, Swift then create an app for Android and iOS devices. Then upload is on various app stores. And monetize your app with Google Admob or by selling them.

Once you’ve developed your app, you can sell it on Flippa for cash through an auction process or a classified listing.

Now a day app development is on the boom so everyone needs an app for their shop or business. So, take advantage of it and make money as a programmer.

3. Start Freelancing

Freelancing = Freedom from 9 to 5 job. The gig economy has seriously changed the game for lots of skilled workers, including you and your genius programming brain.

Just show your programming skills by doing projects of clients online. You can offer your service to the client online.

The awesome thing about freelancing is you can choose your own time, you can choose a project according to your skills, and then you can be done a project anywhere.

Some of the best freelancing platforms include Upwork, Freelancer, and Fiverr. On these sites, you have the opportunity to bid for different projects.

You can also find clients through social media platforms pitching directly to clients that need a programmer on their team is an awesome way to find high-paying clients.

In freelancing, you need to develop communication skills to understand the client’s requirements and for negotiations.

So, start freelancing and make huge money from programming skills.

4. Start YouTube Channel

Start YouTube channel and share your programming knowledge as a form of the video tutorial. YouTube is the best platform to earn money as a programmer.

Now a day, every beginner wants the best mentor to learn to program. And YouTube makes it easier. Everyone prefers to watch videos to understand programming concepts.

YouTube is the largest and most visited site in the world. So, you can definitely earn from YouTube by starting a programming channel.

As we know people like to gain free knowledge so make video tutorial series for them of any programming language.

Ways to earn from YouTube by

  • Google AdSense
  • Sponsorships
  • Selling premium courses

YouTube channel takes time to grow, once you make enough subscriber base then you can make huge money from YouTube.

5. Start a Podcast

Many people don’t like to create videos or don’t want to come in front of the camera. Don’t worry you can still make money by providing knowledge of programming through the audio format means by starting a podcast.

The podcast is an amazing medium to share knowledge as a form of audio. Some people don’t have time to watch videos, so they prefer to listen the podcasts while they driving, walking, or with something else work. So, podcast gained massively in popularity in the last four to five years

Ways to earn from Podcast by,

  • Sponsorship
  • Affiliate marketing
  • Offering premium podcasts

There are many ways to earn from the podcast but before that, you have to build a popular podcast. Also, you have to invest in some good gear to record amazing episodes. And yes, most important is consistency.

So podcast is one of the best way to make money from programming.

6. Start Instagram Page

I know this is social media but now a day social media are totally business. That’s the reality. Social media has a lot of traffic to get real users for your niche.

Start a programming page on Instagram and share knowledge in the form of Images. You can share short articles, tips, tricks, codes.

Most of the programming pages post memes, also a good option to gain more followers and likes.

Ways to earn from Instagram by,

  • Sponsor-ship
  • Selling your premium courses
  • Redirect users to your blog
  • Promoting other pages

Once you build a user base then there are a number of ways to earn from Instagram. You can redirect your user to any other platform like your blog or YouTube channel to gain traffic.

7. Sell Online Courses

The Online Education industry is Huge. And many of the students want to learn code. So why you can’t take advantage of it? Why shouldn’t you be the one to teach them online?

Create courses online using different learning management systems such as Udemy and Coursera. Then sell them to budding coders who want to learn. You can also sell E-Books.

The best way to offer your premium course by offering some free content on YouTube and Blogger and other social media like Instagram and Facebook.

Many of the platforms and universities hiring online teachers to teach programming. So grab the opportunities and make money as a programmer.

Conclusion

These are ways to make money from programming on the internet. These ways take time to grow and yes consistency and quality content is the key to grow on the internet. So, these are the ways to make money from coding. Share it with your friends. Thank you.
